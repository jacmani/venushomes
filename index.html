Questions

Frequently asked

This service is exclusively for Venus Tower. Owners listing for rent or sale, tenants looking for a flat, and buyers searching in Venus Tower are all welcome to call the helpdesk.
Nothing. Owners pay zero commission for both rental and sale listings. For rental, the 50% service fee is collected from the incoming tenant. For sale, the 1% commission is charged to the buyer.
The tenant pays 50% of the agreed monthly rent as a one-time service fee at the time of signing the rental agreement. This is separate from any security deposit paid directly to the owner.
The buyer pays 1% of the final agreed sale value as a service fee. For example, for a flat sold at ₹75 lakhs, the service fee is ₹75,000 — paid by the buyer, not the owner.
